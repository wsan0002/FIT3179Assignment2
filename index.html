World Happiness Score in 2020

by Wilbert Santosa

The World Map

The happiness score in the world is vary. Most of the countries have an average score between 5-6. Only few countries that have a really high happiness score. It can be seen from this map that region that have a high happiness score is Canada, part of Europe, and Ocenia

Who is the happiest and not happpiest country ?

From this dot plot, it can be seen that Finland has the highest happiness score, while Afghanistan has the lowest happiness score

How happy is the world?

From this histogram, it can be seen that most of the countries has the average score in the range of 5-6, which is pretty good. Unfortunately, there are still few countries that have a low happiness score

What determines the happiness score?

From this heatmap, it can be oberserved that there is a positive correlation between social support, life expectancy, and the happiness score. Countries with a high happiness score tend to have a big number of social support and long life expectancy. Meanwhile, countries that have a low happiness score tend to have a small number of social support and short life expectancy

What distinguish happy and not happy country?

From this bubble plot, we can see that main difference between country with high happiness score are the social support number and life expectancy. Freedom to make life choices number seem to not really affect the happiness score
